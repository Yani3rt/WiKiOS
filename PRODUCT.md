# Product

## Register

product

## Users

WikiOS is primarily for individual Obsidian users exploring and maintaining a personal knowledge vault. They use it to search notes, understand relationships between ideas, and recover the wider context around material they have already captured.

## Product Purpose

WikiOS turns a local Obsidian-compatible vault into a searchable, browsable web application. Success means users can move quickly from a note or question to the surrounding knowledge network, with the graph providing an understandable overview rather than requiring repeated hovering and zooming to identify its contents.

## Brand Personality

Calm, editorial, exploratory. The interface should feel considered and readable while encouraging discovery without becoming visually noisy.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic analytics-dashboard styling, decorative graph visualizations that obscure information, and sparse node maps whose labels or relationships are only understandable after hovering or extreme zooming. Avoid novelty effects that compete with the user's knowledge.

## Design Principles

1. Reveal the whole knowledge landscape before asking for interaction.
2. Make relationships and hierarchy legible at a glance.
3. Reward exploration without hiding essential information behind hover states.
4. Preserve the calm reading experience across dense and sparse vaults.
5. Keep the local-first tool focused on the user's notes rather than decorative chrome.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use WCAG 2.2 AA as the technical baseline. Core graph information and actions should be available without relying exclusively on pointer hover, color, fine motor precision, or motion. Respect reduced-motion preferences and maintain clear keyboard focus and readable contrast.
